About Avid Colors

Welcome to Avid Colors, where you can explore the world of colors and learn about color theory.

Our Mission

At Avid Colors, our mission is to help people understand the power and beauty of colors. We provide resources, products, and guides to help you explore the colorful world around us.

About the Author

Ian Carlo Laigo is a color enthusiast and the creator of Avid Colors. He has always been fascinated by colors and their impact on our lives. Ian created this website to share his passion for color and help others discover the beauty of colors in their everyday lives.
